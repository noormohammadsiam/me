About Me

Get to know me better

I am Noor Mohammad Siam, a passionate entrepreneur and technology enthusiast from Cumilla, Bangladesh. Currently pursuing my Bachelor's in Computer Science and Engineering at Independent University, Bangladesh (IUB).

As the CEO and Managing Director of Mozammal Hoque Market, I lead strategic development and operations within the Mozammal Hoque Group. My mission is to merge innovation, leadership, and community-driven technology to create meaningful impact in the digital era.

Beyond business, I actively contribute to Bangla Wikipedia and serve as a Google Maps Local Guide to expand open access to knowledge and strengthen digital connectivity.

My professional interests lie in software development, digital strategy, and IT entrepreneurship, with a focus on creating solutions that bridge technology and community needs.
